Fellowship Extension Test Page

Basic Name Detection

John Smith is a great person. John works at the company with Mary Johnson.

Dr. Sarah Wilson and Professor Michael Brown are collaborating on a project.

Possessive Forms

This is John's car. Mary's house is beautiful.

The students' papers were graded by Mr. Davis.

James' presentation was excellent.

Names with Titles

Mr. Robert Taylor met with Mrs. Linda Anderson yesterday.

President Elizabeth Carter announced the new policy.

Sir William Thompson received an honor.

Repeated Names

Alice Cooper sang a song. Alice is very talented. Cooper's music is amazing.

Tom and Jerry are friends. Tom's cat chased Jerry around the house.

Names with Punctuation

Hello, David! How are you? David, please come here.

"Susan," he called, "can you help me?" Susan's response was quick.

Mixed Content

The meeting included George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, and Benjamin Franklin.

Washington's leadership, Jefferson's vision, and Franklin's wisdom shaped the nation.
