Privacy Policy for The Fellowship

Effective Date: 1 June 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Thanks for using The Fellowship browser extension ("the extension"). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines our handling of user data. It is our aim to be completely transparent about what we do with your data.

Our core principle is simple: We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data or user information through The Fellowship extension.

Information We Do Not Collect

The Fellowship extension operates entirely locally on your browser. It does not require access to, nor does it collect, any of the following:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number
  • Browse history, visited websites, or search queries
  • Data you enter into web forms
  • Your IP address or location information
  • Any unique identifiers related to you or your device
  • Usage statistics or analytics about how you interact with the extension or websites

How the Extension Works

The Fellowship is designed to function without needing any external server communication. All operations performed by the extension happen directly within your browser environment.

Data Sharing and Retention

Since we do not collect any data, there is:

  • No Data Sharing: We have no user data to share with third parties, advertisers, or anyone else.
  • No Data Retention: We have no user data to store or retain.

Permissions

During installation, the browser may indicate that the Extension requires certain permissions to function. Specifically, the extension requests the "storage" permission (to save your preferences locally) and the "activeTab" permission (to modify content on pages you visit). The storage permission is used to remember whether the extension is enabled, while the activeTab permission allows the extension to read and modify the content of web pages you visit to replace names. These permissions are solely for the local operation of the extension's features within your browser and are not used to collect or transmit any data. We strive to request the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to work as intended.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. As we do not collect user information, we cannot proactively notify you of changes. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the extension after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy. However, our core commitment to not collecting your data will remain.
